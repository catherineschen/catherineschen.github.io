Interactive Discovery of Semantics in Deep Networks
======

**Summer 2017**  
*Advisor: [Dr. David Jensen](https://groups.cs.umass.edu/jensen/)*  
*Graduate Student Mentor: [Sam Witty](https://samwitty.github.io/)*  

As a NSF REU participant, I worked in the [Knowledge Discovery Lab](https://kdl.cs.umass.edu/index.html) at UMass Amherst conducting research on explainable AI. Alongside Sam Witty and Javier Burroni, I examined activations from Deepmind's DNN trained to play Atari Breakout in order to describe the network's reasoning in human understandable terms.
